Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about investing with Golden Leaves

How do I invest in Turan Capital Fund?
To invest, please contact our CEO or Investor Relations team via omg@gl.com.kz to schedule a consultation. After verifying your Professional Client status, you will receive the Offering Memorandum and Subscription Agreement.
What are the minimum investment requirements?
The minimum capital commitment for Turan Capital Fund LP is USD 500,000. Additionally, all investors must qualify as Professional Clients under AIFC rules (e.g., net assets > USD 100,000 for individuals).
How long until I receive a response?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please call our direct line.
Can I visit your office?
Yes, we welcome visits from potential investors and partners. Visits are by appointment only. Please email us to schedule a meeting.
Where can I verify your AFSA license?
You can verify our license status on the AFSA Public Register website by searching for our company name "Golden Leaves Ltd" or license number "AFSA-A-LA-2024-0038".
