About Golden Leaves

Home > About Us

Creating Opportunities for Investors and Businesses

Golden Leaves was established in 2020 to provide high-quality consulting services in finance and investment. Today, we are a fully licensed AIFC fund manager specializing in Exempt Private Equity Funds.

We operate within the Astana International Financial Centre (AIFC), leveraging a stable legal framework based on Common Law principles to offer transparent and secure investment vehicles for professional clients.

2020

Company Founded as a financial consultancy firm.

2024

Obtained CIS License from AFSA (AFSA-A-LA-2024-0038) to manage collective investment schemes.

2025

Launch of Turan Capital Fund LP, focused on SME development in Central Asia.

To contribute to Kazakhstan's economic development by attracting investments and developing private capital in strategically important economic sectors, with a focus on supporting SMEs, consolidating small and medium enterprises, and creating National Conglomerates.
