Distribution Waterfall

Alignment of interests: GP only profits after LPs receive capital + 9% return.

1

Return of Capital

100% of distributions go to Limited Partners until they have received back their total contributed capital.

2

Preferred Return (Hurdle)

100% to Limited Partners until they achieve a 9% annual compounded return on their capital.

3

GP Catch-up

100% to the General Partner until it has received 20% of the total profits distributed in steps 2 and 3.

4

Carried Interest Split

Remaining profits are distributed 80% to LPs and 20% to the GP.
